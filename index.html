A HOLISTIC YET SPECIFIC APPROACH

Ecology, Community, and Economy are often conceived of as separate domains. But in reality, they are nested systems.

When we take action in one domain, it doesn't occur in isolation. Our actions have effects that extend beyond our immediate context. Finding information about these effects is often difficult, especially if one lacks resources or expertise.

Accessible, accurate, and actionable information is crucial to maintaining economically and socially beneficial extractive activity without leaving behind our environment and communities.

PanEcology is a worker-owned ecology information company, making available crucial information about resource extraction and its environmental, social, and economic context.

We envision a world where transparent and truthful information about resource extraction is readily available to all, empowering communities, citizens, investors, companies, and government agencies to make informed choices.

To that end, we are developing user-friendly navigation tools, enabling everyone to ask questions in plain English and explore regions through an intuitive map interface.

HIGH QUALITY

PanEcology ensures only the highest quality, well-cited data. Citations are clear and robust, and sources are transparent and highly reliable.

INDEPENDENT

PanEcology is worker-owned and free from special interest influence. What data is available and how it is made available is not now and will never be dependent on moneyed parties.

OPEN

The PanEcology database of raw information is free to access for everyone. Clients may hire analysis services for a fee.

NEUTRAL

We are NOT an advocacy group.

PanEcology does neither advocate any policy nor endorse or oppose any project.
