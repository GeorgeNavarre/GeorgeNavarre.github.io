What We Do

Information and Search

PanEcology.com’s primary purpose is to provide access to information, so that people can more easily make informed decisions and form informed opinions. Click here to access our information portal. Here you will find ecoregional maps that are doorways to regional and site-specific information about the physical and biological nature of a place you are interested in. You will also find a knowledge graph to help your plain language search for more information about a place or a topic. This is where you can start your problem-solving journey.

Network

PanEcology contains two network systems: scientists and public discussion. The network of scientists provides content to the ecoregion map based information system. This network is moderated by fellow scientists to ensure the most accurate available information about a region or site. The public network is a place for knowledgeable members of the public to share with their fellow citizens and for community discussion on a site, project or topic. Click here to enter a moderated conversation about the information on this site.

Edutainment and Simulations

Problem solving can be fun and fun can lead to solving problems. Click here to access our gaming center where you will find problem solving games for pure fun and those posted to explore solutions. This is also a platform for building simulations for problem solving. If you know of a problem that needs imaginative solutions and can be simulated in a game format, let us know. We will work with you to develop a problem-solving game and post it for you. All the solutions will be open source.
