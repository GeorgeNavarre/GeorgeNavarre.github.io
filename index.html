A HOLISTIC YET SPECIFIC APPROACH

Ecology, Community, and Economy are often concieved of as separate domains. But in reality they are nested systems.

When we take action in one domain, it doesn't occur in isolation. Our actions have effects that extend beyond the immediate context.

However, each domain presents unique challenges, language, and standards - and solutions must be specific and actionable.
